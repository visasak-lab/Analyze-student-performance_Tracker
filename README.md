## What is Student Performance Analysis?

Student performance analysis is the process of collecting and evaluating student score data to understand the learning progress and academic achievement of students.

## Purpose

The goal is not only to see the final grade, but to find patterns such as:

* Strengths
* Weak Areas
* Improvement Over Time

## Why Is It Important?

Student performance analysis helps teachers to:

* Discover students who need support
* Understand which subjects are most challenging
* Improve teaching methods
* Give better feedback to parents and students
